Grave Goods began as a private collection — objects gathered from estate sales, antique fairs, and the forgotten corners of old houses. Objects with weight. Objects with histories we may never fully know.

We believe in the beauty of impermanence, the depth of the darkness, and the strange power of objects that outlive their owners. Every piece we carry was chosen for its unique character — it's capacity to hold memory, mystery, and meaning.

This is not mass-market inventory. These are artifacts. Curated, considered, and occasionally haunted. When something sells out, it is gone. That is, perhaps, entirely the point.

Every item we offer is a 'grave good' — a treasure intended to accompany you through this life and perhaps into the next.
